Good to know

Questions travelers ask before ordering.

Is ChinaReady a travel agency?

No. ChinaReady focuses on arrival preparation, practical essentials, hotel delivery, and first-day maps.

Can every hotel receive packages?

Most city hotels can hold guest parcels, but policies vary. The full service should confirm hotel acceptance before shipping.

Do you sell SIM cards, eSIMs, or VPNs?

The homepage can recommend trusted partners later. For the first version, the focus is physical essentials and guidance.

Why include a city map?

Travelers often need Chinese place names, airport routes, hotel districts, and emergency phrases before local apps are fully comfortable.
