These cities cover international arrivals, classic China routes, business districts, and Greater Bay Area border crossings. Each card is a seed version for a full PDF map.

01

Beijing 北京

History, hutongs, embassy areas, and the strongest official English map reference.

PEK / PKXWangfujing 王府井 Qianmen 前门Sanlitun 三里屯
Best arrival zones

Wangfujing, Qianmen, Sanlitun, Chaoyang CBD

First-day route

Airport express or taxi to hotel, then Wangfujing or Qianmen.

Must include

English map link, hutong names, exchange, SIM, transit card, tax refund.

02

可以用支付宝或者微信支付吗？
